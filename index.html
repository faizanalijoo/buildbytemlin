How we guarantee cost reduction?

Minimizing planning vs actual gap

Tons of data come from the site. Work happens in uncontrolled environment leading in unexpected issues and deviation from what was planned. We have developed industry first road specific planning module which helps planning engineers to take corrective actions immediately

Minimizing material production vs application gap

70% of the overrun happen because of material deviation. Be it diesel thefts, wastage in bitumen, over dumping of material etc. We eliminate this problem by showing deviation in material real time by capturing site data accurately

Minimizing machinery idle time

Machines stand idle on chainages due to underusage. With multiple machinery deployed on the site, it is currently difficult to ensure high run rate for all. We solve this issue by sending automatic insights to site office at a per machine level helping allocation of machinery in the most effective manner
